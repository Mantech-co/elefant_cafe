At Elefant Café, we believe healthy food should never be boring. Every dish is crafted with wholesome ingredients, bold flavours, and the pure goodness of plants.

From South Indian Dosa to Bombay Sandwiches, we bring the world's finest plant-based traditions to Milton Keynes — affordably, beautifully, and without compromise.

Our Story

Rooted in community,
grown with purpose

Elefant Café was born from a simple belief: plant-based food should be accessible, affordable, and genuinely delicious. We set up in the heart of Winterhill, Milton Keynes, to bring vibrant wholesome food to our community — without the premium price tag.

We carry a 5-star Food Standards Agency hygiene rating and are proud of the community we've built around good food. From Dosa to Bombay Sandwiches — every item is a tribute to the world's greatest plant-based traditions.
