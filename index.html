Our Story

Rooted in community,
grown with purpose

Elefant Café was born from a simple belief: great food should be accessible, affordable, and genuinely nourishing. We set up in Winterhill, Milton Keynes, to bring vibrant, wholesome takeaway food to our community — without the premium price tag.

We carry a 5-star Food Standards Agency hygiene rating and are proud of the community we've built. Every dish is a tribute to the world's greatest culinary traditions — made for everyone to enjoy.
